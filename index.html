Welcome to The Organise Co.

The Organise Co. is your reliable partner for designing beautifully functional spaces. Operating in Kolkata, India, our organisation offers personal organising and space consultancy services to clients nationwide.

It's the brainchild of Pragati Agarwala and, through the initiative, helps create order from chaos, guiding people to live with joy once more in their neat and organised abode- be it the house, the office, or one's own possessions.

At The Organise Co. we don't just organise the space, but we create experience that enhances lifestyles.

Our Services

We offer a variety of organising solutions designed to meet your unique needs. Here are just a few examples:

  1. Home Organisation: We help you create spaces that inspire joy and order-from decluttering your wardrobe to designing an efficient kitchen.

  2. Office Space Planning: Maximise efficiency and aesthetic appeal with professionally organised workspaces.

  3. Customized Storage Solutions: Get tailored advice on storage options to optimise your space.

  4. Moving Help: Be it a new home or office, our team will ensure a hassle-free move with efficient packing and unpacking services.

Our Story

We at The Organise Co. believe clutter-free space will bring clutter out of your head. Founded under the vision to make lives a little easier to handle, The Organise Co. specialises in making living, working and personal spaces gorgeous and beautiful abodes of wonderful order.

Our personal organisation solution helps the people in need reclaim their precious time, makes their work get easier and feel less stressed from daily chaos and life's drama.
